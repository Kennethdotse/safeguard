Report Cyberbullying and Online Abuse

Being bullied or harassed online is never okay. Here are resources to help you report abuse on various platforms:

Report directly to the platform: Most social media apps, gaming networks, and online platforms have reporting mechanisms for cyberbullying and online abuse. Look for "report," "safety center," or similar options within the platform you're using.

Please email us if you would like to suggest any additions or corrections.

Additional Resources:

  • Cyberbullying Research Center: Report a cyberbullying incident to the Cyberbullying Research Center, Ghana.
  • Contact a trained counselor here
  • AccessNow Digital Security HelpLine: Provides real-time technical assistance and advice to various groups facing online threats. Contact them
  • Consumer Reports Security Planner: Learn how to protect yourself online and offline at Consumer Reports Security Planner

Note: We cannot provide contact information for specific platforms as it can change frequently. However, the reporting tools should be readily available within the platform itself.

Remember, you are not alone. There is help available.
